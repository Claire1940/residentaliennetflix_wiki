D'arcy Bloom is a vibrant and multifaceted character in the science fiction comedy series *Resident Alien*. Portrayed by Alice Wetterlund, she is the chief bartender at The 59 in Patience, Colorado, and a former Olympic skier whose dreams were cut short by an injury. D'arcy is known for her fearless spirit, sharp wit, and deep loyalty to her best friend, Asta Twelvetrees. Her character arc explores themes of self-worth, addiction, and finding purpose beyond past failures.

<Section icon={Target} title="Understanding Asta Twelvetrees">
Asta Twelvetrees, portrayed by Sara Tomko, is a pivotal character in the science-fiction comedy series *Resident Alien*. As a nurse in the small town of Patience, Colorado, Asta's life takes an extraordinary turn when she uncovers the true identity of Dr. Harry Vanderspeigle. Her journey is defined by her deep empathy, resilience, and her unwavering loyalty to those she cares about, even when faced with intergalactic threats. Asta's role evolves from a skeptical colleague to Harry's closest confidante and protector, making her an indispensable part of the show's narrative.

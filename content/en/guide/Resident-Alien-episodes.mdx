<Section icon={Tv} title="Overview of Resident Alien Episodes">
*Resident Alien*, the acclaimed science fiction comedy-drama, has captivated audiences across four seasons, from its debut in January 2021 to its conclusion in August 2025. The series, based on the comic book by Peter Hogan and Steve Parkhouse, follows the extraterrestrial Harry Vanderspeigle, portrayed by Alan Tudyk, who crash-lands on Earth with a mission to destroy humanity. However, as he integrates into the small town of Patience, Colorado, he develops a moral dilemma, leading to hilarious and heartfelt adventures.

The show's narrative cleverly blends mystery, comedy, and drama, exploring themes of identity, humanity, and belonging through Harry's unique perspective. His struggles to understand human social cues, coupled with his secret mission and the few who can see his true form, form the core of the series.

### A Brief History of Harry

Harry, an extraterrestrial with an unpronounceable birth name, crash-lands on Earth with the mission to destroy humanity. However, after assuming the identity of Dr. Harry Vanderspeigle in the small town of Patience, Colorado, he begins to develop human emotions and a moral dilemma. His journey involves learning about human culture, solving murders, and defending Earth from other alien threats.

Linda Hamilton, renowned for her iconic role as Sarah Connor in the *Terminator* franchise, brings her formidable presence to the sci-fi comedy series *Resident Alien* as General Eleanor McCallister. Initially introduced as a recurring antagonist, McCallister's character arc evolves throughout the series, transforming her into a complex figure who may eventually become an ally to Harry Vanderspeigle.

Hamilton's casting significantly impacted the character's trajectory. Showrunner Chris Sheridan famously stated, "You can't kill off Linda Hamilton," leading to the character's survival beyond her originally scripted death in the first season finale, "Heroes of Patience." <Section icon={Shield} title="General McCallister's Pursuit and Key Moments">
General Eleanor McCallister's primary objective revolves around the pursuit of alien life, specifically Harry Vanderspeigle. Her relentless dedication to uncovering the truth about extraterrestrial presence drives much of her storyline. Throughout the seasons, she consistently poses a threat to Harry, even as their interactions occasionally hint at a more complex relationship.

A memorable moment from Season 4, Episode 3, "Ties That Bind," showcases Hamilton's comedic timing and the show's unique dialogue. In this episode, Harry and D'arcy travel back in time, only to find the sought-after Grey technology already in McCallister's possession. Posing as a waitress in 1970s Maine, McCallister is attempting to prevent her father's suicide. Harry's attempt to get D'arcy to leave by fabricating a "diarrhea" emergency led to a line that Hamilton cherishes.

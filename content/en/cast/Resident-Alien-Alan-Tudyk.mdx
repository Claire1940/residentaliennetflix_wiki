<Section icon={Trophy} title="Behind the Scenes: Bringing Harry to Life">
The creation of Harry Vanderspeigle involves more than just Alan Tudyk's acting; it's a collaborative effort that includes practical effects and nuanced direction. For the first season, stuntman Keith Arbuthnot portrayed the alien in his true form.     Tudyk was cast as the lead in September 2018, recognized for his ability to blend comedic timing with dramatic depth.

  <Step num="3" title="Alien Form Portrayal">

The heart of the show lies in its ensemble cast. While Alan Tudyk is the undisputed star, the residents of Patience provide the grounding necessary to make the sci-fi elements work. The chemistry between Harry and Asta Twelvetrees (played by Sara Tomko) is the emotional anchor of the series, showing Harry what it truly means to be human through empathy and shared trauma.

The relationship between Harry and Max is often described as a "Coyote and Roadrunner" dynamic. Harry's constant, failed attempts to "eliminate" the child provide some of the show's funniest moments.

The supporting cast matures significantly over the seasons. For instance, Deputy Liv Baker evolves from a comic relief character into a sharp investigator, often being the only one truly piecing together the strange occurrences in town.
